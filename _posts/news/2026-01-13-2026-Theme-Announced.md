Hear ye, young scholars! Prepare to step through the ancient gates and begin an unforgettable journey, because the theme for our 2026 summer camp is _Curse of the Mystic Waters_!

This summer, campers will be welcomed into a world of wizardry and wonder, where hidden knowledge, mystical challenges, and legendary Scouting lore await. From deciphering enchanted clues to mastering the magic of teamwork, every scout will have the chance to test their abilities, form powerful bonds, and uncover the greatest magic of all!

Campers will dive into a variety of thrilling activities that bring our magical adventure to life, including:

<dl>
  <dt><strong>The Hunt for Jareth's Lost Artifacts</strong></dt>
  <dd>Track down various elusive artifacts from the famed wizard Jareth that have been lost to time! These relics scattered across the realm require the solving of cryptic clues and keen observation to uncover them from their resting places. Only by combining all of their powers will you be able to subdue the Curse that has befallen the realm.</dd>
  <dt><strong>Duel of the Wands</strong></dt>
  <dd>Introduce your magical abilities with a BANG! Showcase this magical prowess across a variety of competitive
matches that require a combination of skill, strategy, and spellcasting to claim victory.</dd>
  <dt><strong>The Gauntlet of Fire</strong></dt>
  <dd>Wizards and Witches seeking to prove their strength, stamina, and skill will be put to the test through many trials around the reservation. The Gauntlet of Fire challenges all spellcasters to a dash through the enchanted forrest, a crossing over the mystic waters, and a precision test that would stump Merlin himself. Join us and see you have the courage, endurance, and magical skills to conquer the flame.</dd>
</dl>

Whether you're mastering new spells, deciphering ancient runes, or taking on thrilling camp challenges, one thing is certain - this will be a summer you won't want to miss. So grab your spellbook, practice your most impressive incantation, and prepare to begin your journey into _Resica Falls: The Curse of the Mystic Waters!_
